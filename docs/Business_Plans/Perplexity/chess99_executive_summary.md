**Your Competitive Advantages:**
1. **India-first:** Pricing in INR, timings for Indian schools, Razorpay support [file:1]
2. **Learning + Play + Compete:** Integrated ecosystem (vs Chess.com's play-first, vs Lichess's free-only) [web:5][web:18]
3. **Teacher Network Model:** Leverage existing school ecosystem (your unique moat) [web:23][web:40]
4. **B2B Potential:** Schools + academies revenue (Chess.com largely B2C) [web:8][web:24]
5. **Founder-founder relationship:** You're credible educator/developer, not just a platform

## REVENUE MODEL (3 Streams)

### 1. **Subscriptions** (Target: 24% of revenue)
- Free tier: Play, basic learning, guest mode [file:1]
- Chess99 Club: ₹99/month, ₹899/year
- Family plan: ₹299/month (3 child accounts + 1 parent dashboard)
- Expected by Month 12: 1,500+ paid users = ₹1,40,000/month [web:2][web:12]

### 2. **Tournaments & credits** (Target: 36% of revenue)
- Platform rake: 20% of entry fees (for example, 10–15% of the prize pool as platform fee, rest as prizes) [file:1][web:10]
- Entry-fee tournaments with fixed or percentage rake
- In-game credit system: parents top up credits; kids spend on tournament entries
- Expected by Month 12: ₹2,00,000/month

### 3. **School B2B** (Target: 20% of revenue)
- Revenue share on school tournaments (15% rake)
- Curriculum integration package: ₹5,000-15,000/month per school [web:8][web:11]
- Teacher commission: ₹50-100 per student signup [web:23][web:26]
- Expected by Month 12: 20+ schools, ₹1,40,000/month recurring

## ACQUISITION STRATEGY (4-Channel Approach)

### Channel 1: Teacher Network (40% of users)
- **How:** Referral program, ₹50-100 per signup + ₹2,000 monthly stipend [web:23][web:26]
- **Scale:** 10 teachers (Week 4) → 50+ teachers (Month 12)
- **CAC:** ₹75 (very efficient)
- **Advantage:** Local trust, high-quality users (already in school system)

### Channel 2: School Partnerships (25% of users)
- **How:** Free tournament pilots → paid partnerships [web:8][web:24]
- **Scale:** 1-2 pilot schools (Week 4) → 20+ schools (Month 12)
- **CAC:** ₹50-100 (bulk signups)
- **Advantage:** 50-500 student signups per school

### Channel 3: Paid Ads (25% of users)
- **How:** Google, Facebook, YouTube targeting parents + kids
- **Budget:** ₹0 (Month 1) → ₹50,000/month (Month 12)
- **CAC:** ₹150-250 initially, optimize to ₹100-150
- **Channels:** Google Ads (keyword), FB/IG (interest), YouTube (pre-roll)

### Channel 4: YouTube & Content (10% of users)
- **How:** 2-3 videos per week on learning, tournaments, chess tips [web:25][web:28]
- **Target:** 50,000+ subscribers by Month 12, organic traffic
- **CAC:** ₹0 (organic), high-quality users (converters)
- **Advantage:** Compounds over time, long-term moat
