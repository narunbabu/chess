## YOUR SPECIFIC BUSINESS MODEL

### Acquisition: 4-Channel Approach

**Channel 1: Teacher Referrals (40% of users)**  
- 10 teachers (Week 4) → 50+ teachers (Month 12)  
- Compensation: ₹75-100 per signup + ₹2,000/month stipend  
- CAC: ~₹75  
- 1 teacher brings 20 students → ₹2,000-3,000 commission  

**Channel 2: School Partnerships (25% of users)**  
- 1 pilot school (Week 4) → 20+ schools (Month 12)  
- Revenue share: 85% school / 15% chess99 (after free pilot)  
- Each school: 50-500 signups potential  

**Channel 3: Paid Ads (25% of users)**  
- Start: ₹300/day total  
- Scale to: ₹2-3L/month by Month 12  
- Channels: Google Search, Facebook/Instagram, YouTube pre-roll  
- CAC: ₹150-250 (optimize down over time)  

**Channel 4: YouTube & Content (10% of users)**  
- 2-3 videos per week  
- Target: 50,000+ subscribers by Month 12  
- CAC: ₹0 (organic)  

### Revenue: 3 Primary Streams + Misc

**1. Subscriptions (24%)**  
- Chess99 Club: ₹99/month, ₹899/year  
- Family Plan: ₹299/month (3 kids + 1 parent)  
- Target: 1,500 paid users = ₹1,44,000/month  

**2. Tournaments (36%)**  
- Platform fee: 20% of entry fees  
- Entry: ₹50-500 per event  

**3. School B2B (20%)**  
- Revenue share on internal school tournaments  
- Fixed: ₹5,000-15,000/month per school  

**4. Misc (20%)**  
- In-app credits  
- Coaching marketplace (15% commission)  
- Affiliate (books, boards, etc.)  

### Branding: “Make in India” Edge

Core positioning:
- “Chess99 – India’s Safe, Affordable Chess Platform for Kids”  
- “Built by Indians, for Indian Kids”  

Messaging:
- Chess improves logical thinking and helps in math/science  
- Indian pricing, Indian timings, Indian languages (future)  
- Emphasis on safe, supervised environment
