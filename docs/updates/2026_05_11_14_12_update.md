# Update: Server-Side Move Validation

## Summary

- Fixed a game-rule bug where castling could remain available after the king moved if the backend accepted a stale client `next_fen`.
- `GameRoomService` now validates multiplayer and synthetic moves from the server's current FEN before persisting them.
- The backend now stores a server-computed FEN and normalizes castling rights after king moves, rook moves, and captures on rook starting squares.
