# Chess99 Academy - Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** December 7, 2025 **Last Updated:** December 7, 2025 ## 1. Commitment to Child Privacy Chess99 Academy is committed to protecting the privacy of children and students who use our educational platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in compliance with: - Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012 - Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines) Rules, 2021 - Right to Education Act provisions - Personal Data Protection Bill principles - COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) guidelines ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Account Registration Information - Child's name, age, and educational level - Parent/guardian contact information - School information (where applicable) - Age verification documentation ### 2.2 Educational Data - Chess learning progress and skill level - Lesson completion and quiz results - Tournament participation and performance - Practice session analytics - Learning patterns and recommendations ### 2.3 Technical Information - Device information for platform compatibility - Login timestamps and session duration - Error logs and technical performance data - IP address (masked for privacy) ### 2.4 Communication Data - Messages with teachers and support staff - Parent-teacher communication records - Feedback and improvement suggestions ## 3. How We Use Information ### 3.1 Educational Purposes - Personalizing learning experiences - Tracking educational progress - Recommending appropriate lessons - Creating skill-based matchings - Generating progress reports ### 3.2 Platform Operations - Maintaining account security - Providing technical support - Improving educational content - Ensuring platform stability - Preventing cheating and fraud ### 3.3 Safety and Compliance - Verifying age and identity - Monitoring for inappropriate behavior - Responding to safety concerns - Compliance with educational regulations ## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure ### 4.1 We DO NOT Share or Sell: - Personal information to third parties for marketing - Student data with advertisers - Learning progress with unauthorized parties - Contact information to commercial entities ### 4.2 Limited Sharing With: - **Parents/Guardians:** Full access to their child's educational data - **Educational Partners:** Only with explicit parental consent - **Schools:** Aggregate, anonymized performance data - **Legal Authorities:** When required by law for child protection ### 4.3 Service Providers We use trusted third-party services that: - Sign strict data protection agreements - Comply with child privacy laws - Use data only for specified purposes - Maintain appropriate security measures ## 5. Data Security Measures ### 5.1 Technical Protections - End-to-end encryption for sensitive data - Secure servers with restricted access - Regular security audits and penetration testing - Multi-factor authentication for admin access - SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission ### 5.2 Organizational Protections - Staff background checks and training - Privacy policies for all employees - Limited data access on need-to-know basis - Regular privacy compliance audits - Incident response procedures ### 5.3 Child-Specific Protections - No collection of unnecessary personal information - Strict age verification procedures - Monitoring for inappropriate contact attempts - Easy reporting mechanisms for concerns - Parental oversight and control features ## 6. Parental Rights and Controls ### 6.1 Access Rights Parents have the right to: - Review all information collected about their child - Request corrections to inaccurate information - Download their child's educational data - Delete their child's account and data ### 6.2 Control Features - Set privacy preferences for their child's profile - Control communication settings - Approve or decline educational partnerships - Monitor learning progress and activity ### 6.3 Consent Management - Explicit consent required for data collection - Easy withdrawal of consent at any time - Clear explanations of data use purposes - Age-appropriate privacy notices ## 7. Data Retention and Deletion ### 7.1 Retention Periods - **Account Data:** Until account deletion or request for removal - **Educational Progress:** 7 years after account closure (for educational records) - **Technical Logs:** 90 days (for security and performance) - **Communication Records:** 2 years (for safety and compliance) ### 7.2 Deletion Process - Immediate deletion of personal information upon request - Secure data destruction methods - Confirmation of deletion provided to parents - Removal from all active systems and backups ## 8. International Data Transfers Chess99 Academy operates primarily within India and: - Stores all sensitive personal data within India - Complies with Indian data protection laws - Uses only Indian data centers for educational records - Provides local support for privacy concerns ## 9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies ### 9.1 Educational Cookies - Session management for learning progress - Personalization of learning content - Platform functionality and security - Performance analytics and improvements ### 9.2 Parental Control - Clear information about all cookies used - Options to manage cookie preferences - No marketing or advertising cookies - Age-appropriate cookie policies ## 10. Third-Party Links and Integrations ### 10.1 Educational Partners - Only partners who comply with child privacy laws - Explicit parental consent required - Limited data sharing for educational purposes - Regular privacy compliance monitoring ### 10.2 External Resources - Carefully vetted educational content providers - Safe browsing protections - No direct data sharing without consent - Regular security assessments ## 11. Breach Notification In the unlikely event of a data breach: - Immediate notification to affected parents/guardians - Clear explanation of what happened - Steps taken to protect children - Measures to prevent future incidents - Notification to appropriate regulatory authorities ## 12. Privacy Policy Changes ### 12.1 Notification Process - Email notification to all registered parents - Platform notifications for active users - Posted on our website with effective date - 30-day notice period for significant changes ### 12.2 Feedback and Comments We welcome parental feedback on our privacy practices: - Privacy@safety@chess99.com - Dedicated privacy helpline for parents - Regular consultation with child protection experts ## 13.
