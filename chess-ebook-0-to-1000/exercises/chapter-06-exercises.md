## Answers

**6.1:** 1=Opponent's last move — what changed? 2=CCT scan (your turn). 3=Choose candidate move. 4=Blunder check (destination safe? leaving undefended? best reply? discovered attack?). 5=Play the move.

**6.2:** 1=Bishop diagonal (or Queen on same diagonal). 2=Pin (knight pinned to queen, moving it loses the queen). 3=Back rank mate (no luft, king trapped). 4=Knight fork. 5=Trap (poisoned pawn — capturing triggers discovered attack).

**6.3:** Opening=1 min, Early middlegame=3 min, Late middlegame=3 min, Endgame=3 min.

**6.4:** A=2 seconds (forced recapture, no decision). B=30+ seconds (choice matters — compare positions after each capture). C=30+ seconds (suspicious move — find the trap before capturing). D=2 seconds (only one move, play it).
